Norway

Experience the magic of Norway, a land of dramatic fjords, majestic mountains, and the mesmerizing Northern Lights.

Lofoten

Lofoten is a place of contrasts. It’s a natural wonder with thriving local communities. It’s where mountains meet the sea, and where local businesses meet recreation.

  • Scandic Svolvær – 5 km away – ⭐ 4.2 – NOK 1,600/night
  • Thon Hotel Lofoten – 6 km away – ⭐ 4.6 – NOK 1,900/night
  • Nyvågar Rorbuhotell – 8 km away – ⭐ 4.5 – NOK 2,100/night

Northern Lights

The northern lights, also known as the aurora borealis, are one of nature's most spectacular displays.

  • Scandic Ishavshotel (Tromsø) – 0.5 km from city center – ⭐ 4.4 – NOK 2,000/night
  • Clarion Hotel The Edge (Tromsø) – 0.7 km from city center – ⭐ 4.5 – NOK 2,200/night
  • Radisson Blu Hotel Tromsø – 0.6 km from city center – ⭐ 4.3 – NOK 2,100/night

Fjord View

Norway’s fjords are deep, narrow inlets surrounded by towering cliffs and lush landscapes, carved by glaciers over thousands of years.

  • Fretheim Hotel (Flåm) – 0.3 km from Aurlandsfjord – ⭐ 4.4 – NOK 2,000/night
  • Flåmsbrygga Hotel – 0.2 km from Aurlandsfjord – ⭐ 4.3 – NOK 1,900/night
