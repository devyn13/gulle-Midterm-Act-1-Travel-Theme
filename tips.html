Travel Tips

Practical advice to make your journey smoother, safer, and more enjoyable.

🧳 Pack Light

Essentials only!

Bring versatile clothing, use packing cubes, and roll clothes to save space & avoid wrinkles.

💳 Backup Documents

Stay safe!

Always keep both digital & paper copies of your passport, ID, and travel insurance documents.

🌍 Local Phrases

Show respect!

Learning “Hello,” “Thank you,” and “Help” in the local language can build trust and friendships.

🛡️ Safety First

Be prepared!

Avoid displaying valuables, use hotel safes, and keep emergency contacts handy at all times.

📅 Off-Peak Travel

Smart timing!

Travel in spring or fall for cheaper prices, fewer tourists, and more authentic local experiences.
