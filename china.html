China

From iconic landmarks to breathtaking natural wonders, it offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall stretches thousands of kilometers across northern China and is one of the world’s most famous historical sites.

  • Brickyard Retreat at Mutianyu Great Wall – 1 km away – ⭐ 4.7 – ¥1200/night
  • Commune by the Great Wall – 2 km away – ⭐ 4.4 – ¥1500/night
  • Home of the Great Wall – 1.5 km away – ⭐ 4.2 – ¥800/night

Forbidden City

Located in Beijing, the Forbidden City is a magnificent palace complex that served as the home of emperors for centuries.

  • Hotel Kapok Beijing – 0.5 km away – ⭐ 4.3 – ¥900/night
  • Beijing Hotel NUO – 1 km away – ⭐ 4.6 – ¥1300/night
  • Sunworld Dynasty Hotel – 1.2 km away – ⭐ 4.2 – ¥850/night

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

Famous for its towering sandstone pillars, Zhangjiajie inspired the floating mountains in the movie Avatar and offers breathtaking natural scenery.

  • Pullman Zhangjiajie Hotel – 2 km away – ⭐ 4.5 – ¥700/night
  • Qinghe Jinjiang International Hotel – 3 km away – ⭐ 4.4 – ¥650/night
  • Zhangjiajie Sunshine Hotel – 5 km away – ⭐ 4.3 – ¥600/night
